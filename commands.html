Commands

Clans

/clan create | Create a clan for you.

/clan invite | Invites a member to your clan.

/clan leaderboard | Shows the best ranked players.

/clan war | Starts or ends a war with another clan.

/clan land | Shares land with your clan members.

/clan ally | Adds or removes alliances.

/clan home | Adds or removes a clans home.

/clan list | Shows a list of all clans.

/clan profile | Shows information about a clan.

/clan lookup | Shows information about a player.

/clan promote | Promotes a member to leader.

/clan trust | Trusts a member.

/ally {message} | Talk to clan members or allies.

/clan bank | Manages the bank account.

Home

/sethome | Set a home.

/delhome | Delete a home.

/home | Go to your home.

TPA

/tpa | Tpa to a user.

/tpaccept | Accept a tpa request.

/tpacancel | Decline a tpa request.

/tpahere | Send a request for a user to teleport to you.

Others

/back | Go to your last death point.
